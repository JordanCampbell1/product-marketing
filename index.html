What iVoya does

Everything you need, before you even land

iVoya learns how you travel, then quietly handles the parts that usually go wrong.

⚠

Live safety heat map

Areas are marked red, orange, or green in real time — from crash hotspots to neighborhoods needing extra caution — so you always know what's ahead.

🧭

Smart itinerary builder

Search and select, or let iVoya generate a full itinerary based on what it's learned about your tendencies — download it and go, no signal required.

📍

Quick Stop

Need a hairdresser, pharmacy, or pet store right now? Type it in and get trusted, area-specific recommendations in seconds.

Travel Safe notifications
